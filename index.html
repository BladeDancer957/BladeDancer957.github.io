Biography

My Chinese name is 张(Zhang)笃(Du)振(zhen). I am a PhD student in artificial intelligence at CASIA advised by Bo Xu and Tielin Zhang. Previously, my research interests included Emotion Analysis in Conversations, Incremental/Federated Learning and Its Applications in Information Extraction, and Brain­-inspired Intelligence. Now, I'm currently working on Continual Learning in LLMs, Large Multi-Modal Model, and Applications of LLMs.

I am looking for cooperation. Contact me if you are interested in the above topics.

I am actively seeking a postdoctoral position as well. If you are interested in my experience, please feel free to contact me and let me know.
